ABOUT ME

ON THE WAY TO THE TOP

Hi! My name is Iveta Zeltiņa. I am an explorer. I perceive life as a cognitive process that gives me the opportunity to get to know myself, others and the world. Interesting - my guiding principle in life. Direction is my way of getting energy and transforming it into things that matter to me. Without limits, or not setting a frame for yourself - my approach to things that allows you not to be afraid to look outside the box, experiment and gain new useful experience. Consciousness – to do things consciously, in accordance with what is valuable and constantly reflect. Acceptance - to accept the different, even if it is not in accordance with my understanding of things and values. Realize that the world is different and people are different.


My understanding of things is neither better nor worse - it's just mine. Be happy and wonder - every little thing experienced every day should be appreciated. People - communication with others allows me to get to know yourself better and learn not only to take, but also to give. Learning is interesting, so it is worth doing throughout your life, because only an interesting life is a valuable life. Without fear - life is a free flight and a continuous process of self-discovery. It is not worth regretting the lost, because the world is full of opportunities. They must be seen, chosen and enjoyed in the process of life's flight. Process - life should be seen as a process, not a beginning and an end, then it is easy to achieve a sense of fulfillment and happiness. No expectations - nobody deserves anything, I have to appreciate what is given. To do - everything is only in your own hands. Enjoy life on the way to the top!
